Our Foundation Story

FreeTheRealFoundation was forged in the fire of struggle and resolve. Its founder, Elijah Stroman, was a man of uncommon insight. He spent many years behind bars, doing everything he could to learn and grow. But when freedom came, it came with systems and barriers that felt impossible to navigate.

His dream was one of support, clarity, and hope—a path that others could follow without stumbling through the unknown. This foundation is for the real ones—those rebuilding, rewiring, and reclaiming their future.

Our Mission

FreeTheRealFoundation is committed to transforming the lives of incarcerated individuals by equipping them with real-world life skills, educational tools, and personal development strategies for successful reintegration into society. We honor the legacy of Elijah Stroman by fostering empowerment, integrity, inclusion, and transformation within correctional environments and beyond.

We provide comprehensive support, education, and advocacy for individuals reentering society after incarceration. Our programs focus on practical skills, emotional support, and community integration to ensure successful transitions and reduced recidivism.

About Elijah Stroman

Elijah Stroman's journey from incarceration to empowerment exemplifies the transformative power of second chances. After experiencing firsthand the challenges of reentry, Elijah dedicated his life to ensuring others wouldn't face those struggles alone. His vision became the foundation for our comprehensive support system that addresses the real-world needs of individuals transitioning back into society.

Programs & Resources

Our programs are designed to address the full spectrum of reentry challenges: from practical skills like job readiness and financial literacy, to emotional support through mentorship and counseling. We believe in treating each individual holistically, recognizing that successful reintegration requires more than just finding employment—it requires rebuilding lives, relationships, and hope.
