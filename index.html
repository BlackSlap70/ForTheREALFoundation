Our Mission

FREE the REAL Foundation is committed to transforming the lives of incarcerated individuals by equipping them with real-world life skills, educational tools, and personal development strategies for successful reintegration into society. We honor the legacy of Elijah Stroman by fostering empowerment, integrity, inclusion, and transformation within correctional environments and beyond.

We provide comprehensive support, education, and advocacy for individuals reentering society after incarceration. Our programs focus on practical skills, emotional support, and community integration to ensure successful transitions and reduced recidivism.
