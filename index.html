Our Mission

FREE the REAL Foundation is committed to transforming the lives of incarcerated individuals by equipping them with real-world life skills, educational tools, and personal development strategies for successful reintegration into society.

Community Impact

Our comprehensive approach has helped hundreds of individuals successfully reintegrate into their communities, reducing recidivism and building stronger families and neighborhoods.

Programs & Resources

Our programs are designed to address the full spectrum of reentry challenges: from practical skills like job readiness and financial literacy, to emotional support through mentorship and counseling.
